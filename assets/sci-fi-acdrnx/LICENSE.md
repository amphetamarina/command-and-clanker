## What isotop uses from this pack (v0 scope)

- `sci-fi/buildings/Step {1,2,3}/{foerderturm,kraftwerk,raffinerie,treibhaus}.png`
  - The four single-tile-wide buildings across the three colour variants
    (12 unique sprites).
- `sci-fi/background/Tileset.png`
  - The 6 ground diamond tiles.

## What is in the pack but unused by v0

- The 2-tile-wide buildings (`kaserne`, `raktensilo`, `werkstatt`,
  `hauptgebaeude`). v0 only uses 1-tile-wide buildings to keep the grid
  layout uniform; mixed-footprint placement is a v1 problem.
- The `Destruction/` and `Door Animation/` building frames. Reserved
  for when v0 grows live state (process exit, etc.).
- All of `sci-fi/units/` (Mech, Mech2, Soldiers, car, drone). Reserved
  for v1 NPCs.
- `sci-fi/interface/`. v0 has no in-game UI.

The full pack is kept in the tree because it is small (~700 KB) and
keeping unused assets co-located simplifies the v1 work.
